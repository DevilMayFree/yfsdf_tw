Graphic and Packaging Services

Jupiter Group was established in 1996 as a packaging design and management company for retailers in the US. In 2009, the international sales department of YFY’s Packaging Inc. subsidiary merged with the Jupiter Group to form YFY Jupiter. The company serves customers and operates offices for professional design and printing management services in Greater China, Singapore, the US, Mexico, the UK, and Australia. Many of its clients are major international enterprises, such as Dell, HP, ASUS, Google, Best Buy, Wal-Mart, Target, Lowes, P&G, Nestlé, and Kraft.

YFY Jupiter specializes in providing customer solutions through innovative technological products. Upholding the principle of “Packaging Matters”, the company emphasizes the importance of packaging design to brands and products, offering complete service solutions that include commercial photography, brand packaging design, certification testing, and packaging and printing management services.
