    • Press Release

  • A model for sustainable businesses

    Yuen Foong Yu is one the most iconic companies in Taiwan. Starting out as a papermaker to developing a corporation on a multi-industry scale, it integrates innovation with evolution and value-added services to become one of the leaders of the industry. YFY also promotes a balanced and sustainable environment as well as fulfills its corporate social responsibility by devoting to cultural development and environmental protection.

  • The zero emission green vision

    Yuen Foong Yu Paper Manufacturing Group is committed to environmental protection in keeping up with the times. YFY has obtained international environmental certifications, it is COC and PEFC™certified in papermaking. The company actively participates in carbon footprint examinations and progresses towards the goal of zero emission and waste recycling.

  • Green innovation, integrated services

    Yuen Foong Yu is a rare corporation with over 90 years of history. Determined to build innovation from its core papermaking business, it has transformed and metamorphosed into creating specialty paper, anti-counterfeiting printing, precision printing, packaging design, and even high-tech electronic paper. From manufacturing to services and products, YFY’s new focus is on innovative material and integrated applications.

  • The perfect packaging solution

    Turning innovative ideas into marketable products requires a lot of fine-tuning. Companies also have to ensure safe delivery to customers. Too much packaging is a waste, too little can damage the product; the key is to strike a good balance. YFY provides sustainable and customized packaging solutions, helping your products achieve added value and better protection.

    More on Containerboard and Packaging Group

  • A carrier of knowledge and ideas

    YFY is the first manufacturer of paper for reading and writing, while Chung Hwa Pulp Corp., a member of the YFY Group, is the first company to achieve vertical integration in the field of pulp production. Over the past 60 years, YFY has successfully developed various specialty papers in an era of rapid economic development in Taiwan. A piece of paper is a carrier for knowledge and new ideas, a product that we cherish and are proud to produce.

    More on Pulp and Fine Paper Group

  • Healthy living with every gentle touch

    After building its reputation as a papermaker, the YFY Group carried its professional papermaking technology into the tissue paper market in 1964. By 2004, YFY had become Taiwan’s largest household paper manufacturer, cementing its position as Taiwan’s market leader. For more than 50 years, YFY has remained committed to the quality of every gentle touch.

    More on Consumer Products Group

  • The leader in natural cleaning products

    Driven by a business philosophy of environmental protection, sustainability and altruism, the YFY Consumer Products Group forged strategic partnerships to develop a natural, non-toxic cleaning agent powered by 100% food-grade, cold-pressed orange oil. The Orange House product range was launched successfully in 2009, spearheading the growth of the “natural” cleaning trend and enabling more households to safely use natural, non-toxic cleaning products.

    More on Consumer Products Group

  • Seeing more with ePaper

    YFY started out as a traditional paper manufacturer, but we were able to greatly increase the value of paper by turning it digital. E Ink, a member of the YFY Group, has a 95% share in the global ePaper market. Its emergence created a new form of delivering information that doesn’t rely on physical paper. EPaper can be used repeatedly and supports a wide range of interactive features, opening up a new world of free and unlimited communication.

    More on Technology Group

  • Advocate of an Organic Lifestyle

    YFY Group’s GREEN&SAFE, not only provides healthy and great tasting food, but also promotes a sustainable lifestyle. The Ayatal tribe has a tradition of planting an alder tree for each newborn, which after 20 years, serves as a coming of age gift. Its by-product—tree-grown mushrooms—becomes important sources of livelihood for these young adults. The planting, harvesting, and baking of these mushrooms embodies the concept of harmony with nature in which we value greatly at YFY.
