Achievements

1992
  • The Xinwu Plant was awarded factory of the year in the categories of air pollution prevention and environmental protection.
  • The Jiutang and Chenggong Plants were awarded factories of the year in the categories of water pollution control and environmental protection.

1993
  • Baihua tissue paper and four other products were among the first recipients of the Green Mark certificate in Taiwan
  • The Taitung Plant received an annual award for outstanding achievements in the field of waste reduction in Taiwan
  • The Taitung Plant received an annual award for outstanding achievements in the field of water pollution prevention in Taitung County
  • The Chenggong Plant received an annual award for outstanding achievements in the field of industrial pollution prevention in Taiwan.

1994
  • The Taitung Plant received an annual award for outstanding achievements in the field of air pollution prevention in Taitung County

1995
  • The Xinwu Plant received an award for power conservation from Taipower
  • The Chenggong Plant received an award for excellence in the field of environmental protection in Taichung County

1996
  • The Fengyuan Plant received an award for outstanding factory from the annual pollution prevention evaluation program by the Environmental Protection Administration
  • The Xinwu Plant received a commendation for outstanding achievements in the field of waste reduction, which is presented by the Industrial Development Bureau of the Ministry of Economic Affairs

1997
  • The Chenggong Plant received the first prize for outstanding waste management facilities among Taichung County Businesses
  • The Chenggong Plant received the ISO14001 certification.

1998
  • The Yangmei, Taoyuan, Kaohsiung, Taitung, and Xinwu Plants received the ISO14001 certification
  • The Chenggong and Taitung Plants received licenses for waste management for Category II businesses
  • The Xinwu Plant received an award for outstanding factory from the annual pollution prevention evaluation program by the Environmental Protection Administration

1999
  • The Xinwu Plant received awards for outstanding achievements in the fields of waste reduction and energy conservation presented by the Ministry of Economic Affairs.
  • All paper manufacturing and paper container plants received the ISO14001 certification

2002
  • The Xinwu Plant received the 2002 excellence award for outstanding achievements in the field of energy conservation, presented by the Ministry of Economic Affairs.

2003
  • The Xinwu Plant received the 2003 award for outstanding achievements in the field of water conservation, presented by the Water Resource Agency of the Ministry of Economic Affairs.

2004
  • YFY received the award for successful digitization in the Asia-Pacific region, presented by the Industrial Development Bureau of the Ministry of Economic Affairs.
  • Mr. S. C. Ho received the 3rd class Economic Achievement Medal awarded by the Ministry of Economic Affairs

2005
  • The Xinwu Plant was selected as an example of excellent safety and hygiene by the Ministry of Economic Affairs in 2005

2007
  • The Jiutang Plant received the 2007 award for outstanding achievements in the field of greenhouse gas reduction, presented by the Ministry of Economic Affairs

2008
  • YFY became the first FSC™ certified (License Code：FSC™ C016878) paper manufacturing company in Taiwan
  • Participation in the program for energy conservation and carbon reduction sponsored by the MoEA (the goal was a total CO₂ reduction of 20,000 tons over a period of three years)
  • The Chenggong Plant received the 2007 award for outstanding achievements in the field of greenhouse gas reduction

2009
  • YFY's paper production related business sectors, Shen's Art Printing Co., Ltd. and China Color Printing Co., Ltd. all became FSC™ certified (License Code：FSC™ C006330...), creating a fully FSC™ certified production chain.

2010
  • YFY received the choice award for best listed companies, presented by the China Credit Information Service, Ltd.
  • The Taoyuan Plant received the National Brand Yushan Award for best product.
  • Yuen Foong Yu Family Care (Kunshan) received the following awards during the 8th National Congress of TnPM: Information Applications, corporate OPL systems, corporate creative visual management, and corporate improvement in six case studies.

2011
  • The Taitung Plant received the energy conservation and carbon reduction badge, awarded by the Environmental Protection Administration
  • YFY's "Mayflower" brand was selected as one of the top 100 brands in Taiwan
  • YFY's "Mayflower" brand was selected as one of the top 100 brands in Taiwan
  • YFY's Qingshui Plant received the health promotion badge, awarded by the National Health Department
  • PEK Crown paper Co,. Ltd received 2nd place in the cardboard category of the 5th annual Golden Print Award

2012
  • Orange House products were voted the number one brand in both laundry detergent and dish soap categories by Common Health Magazine.
  • YFY Paper Manufacturing Co. was awarded the top 80 Innovative Enterprises of 2011 by the Ministry of Economic Affairs.
  • YFY Taoyuan Plant received first place in the printed cardboard box category of the 6th annual Golden Print Award.
  • PEK Crown paper Co,. Ltd received 2nd and 3rd place in the printed cardboard box category of 6th annual Golden Print Award.
  • YFY Changhua Plant, PEK Crown paper Co., Ltd received the National Brand Yushan Award for best product.

2013
  • YFY Changhua Plant, PEK Crown paper Co., Ltd received the National Brand Yushan Award.
  • Orange House products were voted the number one brand in both laundry detergent and dish soap categories by Common Health Magazine.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp passed ISO 50001 Energy Management System Certification

2014
  • YFY's nPulp (straw based packing) wins silver medal in San Francisco at the Edison Awards for the Energy & Sustainability.

2015
  • The Yuen Foong Hsin-Yi building, YFY’s Taipei Headquarters, was recognized with a Taipei Energy Conservation Leadership Award and Commercial Building Energy Conservation Certification from the Taipei City Government.
  • YFY’s Containerboard and Packaging Group received its 6th National Brand Yushan Award for “Best Product”.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp’s Jiutang Plant was ranked first in the annual list of green procurements issued by Kaohsiung’s Environmental Protection Bureau.

2016
  • Yangzhou’s nPulp plant received a 2016 World Green Design Grand Award Silver Award issued by the WGDO.
  • YFY received Green Leadership Award of 2016 Asia Responsible Entrepreneurship Awards for CHP’s “Rediscovery of Hydrogen Energy” project taken place in Hualien Mill.
  • YFY’s Xinwu Plant commenced long-term participation in river adoption program, receiving an Excellency Award.
  • Changhua and Taoyuan Plants participated in the 3rd Package Design Awards, earning 2nd place and an honorable mention in the society category, respectively.
  • The Yuen Foong Hsin-Yi building is awarded 2016 Taipei Energy Conservation Leadership Awards for both energy and electricity saving efficiency.
  • YFY’s industrial paper machine received a National Brand Yushan Award for best product for the 7th year running.
  • YFY received a 2015 Energy Conservation Award from the Ministry of Economic Affairs.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp Corp’s Hualien Mill was named to the 2016 Industrial GHG Voluntary Reduction Merit List by the Industry Development Bureau, Ministry of Economic Affairs.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp Corp’s Jiutang Mill was awarded a Green Procurement Award issued by the Kaohsiung’s Environmental Protection Bureau.
  • YFY’s Yangmei Plant is awarded a Health Promotion Excellency Award by the Taoyuan Municipal Health Bureau.

2017
  • The Qingshui Plant received the Energy-saving Benchmark Silver Award from the Ministry of Economic Affairs, once again earning major national recognition for YFY Consumer Group's business philosophy of energy conservation and sustainability. As the production base of Mayflower tissue paper, the Qingshui Plant won the 2017 Silver Award from a large pool of corporate competitors for its outstanding energy efficiency.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp Corp received two Taiwan Corporate Sustainability Awards — the "Top 50 Traditional Manufacturer Corporate Sustainability Award" and the "Traditional Manufacturer CSR Report Bronze Award" — earning recognition for its environmental protection and sustainability business philosophy.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp Corp was commended as an "Outstanding Green Procurement Organization" by the Taipei City Government for actively promoting "green sustainability" in response to the government's green procurement policy and accumulating NT$1.2 billion in green procurements between January and October 2017.

2018
  • YFY received the Taiwan Corporate Sustainability Award – Gold Award.
  • YFY began implementing a healthy workplace promotion program at all business locations across Taiwan and received the “Healthy Workplace Certification” from the Health Promotion Administration, Ministry of Health and Welfare.
  • YFY Headquarters, Yangmei Plant and Qingshui Plant certified as “AED Safe Areas”.

2019
  • YFY received the 16th National Brand Yushan Award – Best Product and the 13th Taiwan Golden Print Awards – First Prize, Packaging Carton Category.
  • YFY received the 2019 National Healthy Workplace – Innovative Workplace Award.
  • YFY received the 2019 Taiwan iSports Certification.
  • YFY received the 2019 Taiwan Corporate Sustainability Award – Gold Award.

2020
  • YFY received the Global Views Corporate Social Responsibility Award – Model Award, Environmentally-Friendly Category.
  • YFY Industrial Paper’s Xinwu Plant became the first company in the global paper industry to acquire BS 8001 Circular Economy certification.
  • YFY Consumer Products’ Yangmei Plant received the Ministry of Economic Affairs’ 2020 Energy-saving Benchmark Award, the 2nd Annual Enterprise Environmental Protection Award – Bronze Award, Manufacturing Category, and the Taiwan iSports Certification.
  • YFY Consumer Products’ Qingshui Plant was named an “Outstanding Green Procurement Organization” of the 2020 Green Living & Green Consumption campaign of the Taichung City Environmental Protection Bureau.
  • YFY Inc., YFY Consumer Products, and YFY Industrial Paper each became a Common Health Magazine CHR (Corporate Social Responsibility) Pledge Company.
  • YFY Inc. advanced the Ministry of Labor’s efforts to strengthen the promotion of workplace health management, becoming the “YFY Healthy Family” Excellent Model Enterprise.
  • YFY Corporate Advisory & Services Co., Ltd. received the “Badge of Accredited Healthy Workplace” issued by the Health Promotion Administration, Ministry of Health and Welfare, and the Anti-epidemic Vanguard Silver Award.
  • Chung Hwa Pulp Corp. was named a Green Procurement – Excellent Organization, recognized as an Air Quality Purification Zone adoptee for the fourth consecutive year, and won the Asia Responsible Enterprise Award – Circular Economy Award.
